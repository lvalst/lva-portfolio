PROJECTS

brief biology quiz

  • Live App
  • Repo

The Brief Biology Quiz asks five high-school biology questions in the format of a scored single multiple-choice question and, on submission, displays the correct answer and possible added point.

At the appearance of the answer, the user is prompted to click for the next question and this format continues until the results page. Upon completion, the site shows the user’s total score, the number of questions, and offers a button to retry the quiz from the beginning.
